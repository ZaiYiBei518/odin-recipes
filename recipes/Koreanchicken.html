Korean Style Fried Chicken

Description

Here is a recipe to make Korean style fried chicken at home.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable Oil
  • 1 tsp Kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 lb chicken wings
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 5 dried red chilis
  • 1 tbsp minced ginger
  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • 1/4 cup gochujang
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocktail peanuts
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 1 green onion, sliced

Steps

  1. Make wings: In a deep pot over medium-high heat, heat 4 to 6 cups of vegetable oil to 275 degrees.
  2. Line a large plate or baking sheet with paper towels. In a bowl, combine salt, black pepper, baking powder, and garlic powder
  3. Pat wings dry. Rub with ginger and salt. In a large bowl, mix the wings with cornstarch.
  4. Add wings and fry until the skin is chrisp. Remove the wings and set aside on a plate.
  5. Heat oil to 400 degrees. Return the wings and cook for 7 to 8 minutes. Remove the wings then place them into a large mixing bowl.
  6. Make the sauce.
  7. Pour the sauce over the wings.
  8. Garnish the wings with peanut, sesame seeds, and green onion.
