Fried Rice

Description

Here is a quick and easy recipe to make Chinese fried rice at home.

Ingredients

  • Cooked, Chilled Rice
  • Eggs
  • Carrots, Onions, Green Onions, and Peas
  • Minced Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Toasted Sesame Oil
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Scramble the eggs with a small pat of butter. Set aside when finished.
  2. Sautee the vegetables with garlic
  3. Stir fry the rice on high heat for about three minutes with the vegetables
  4. Take the pan off the heat and add green onions, sesame oil, and scrambled eggs.
  5. Season the rice with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
