Ajiaco (Colombian Chicken Soup)

Description

Here is the recipe for Ajiaco, a Colombian chicken soup

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken breasts
  • 12 cups of water
  • 3 ears of corn
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 chicken bouillon cubes
  • 3 scallions
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 3 tablesppons of chopped cliantro
  • 2 cups of papa criolla
  • 3 medium white potatoes
  • 3 medium red potatoes
  • 1 cup of heavy cream for serving
  • 1 cup of capers for serving

Steps

  1. In a large pot, place the chicken, corn, chicken bouillon, cilantro, scallions, garlic, salt, and pepper. Add water and bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to medium and cook for 30 to 35 minutes, until the chicken is cooked and tender. Remove the chicken and set aside.
  2. Continue cooking the corn for about 15 more minutes. Discard the green onion then add the red and white potatoes. Cook for 15 more minutes.
  3. Uncover then add the frozen papa criolla and simmer for 15 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Cut the chicken breasts into small pieces then return the chicken into the pot. Serve the Ajiaco hot with the heavy cream and capers on the side.
